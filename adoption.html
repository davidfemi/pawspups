ADOPTION PROCESS

Our adoption process is designed to create successful, lifelong matches between dogs and their new families. The journey begins with an application where we learn about your lifestyle, experience with pets, and what you're looking for in a canine companion. Next, we'll arrange a meet-and-greet with potential matches, followed by a home visit to ensure your space is safe and suitable. Once approved, you'll complete the adoption paperwork, pay the adoption fee, and welcome your new best friend home!

AVAILABLE DOGS

We have a variety of wonderful dogs waiting for their forever homes. From playful puppies to wise seniors, from tiny lap dogs to energetic large breeds, our available dogs come in all shapes, sizes, and personalities. Each dog in our care has been evaluated by our veterinary and behavioral teams, and we're committed to helping you find the perfect match for your family. Visit our facility to meet our current residents or check our website regularly for updates on newly available dogs.

ADOPTION FEES

Our adoption fees range from $150 to $350, depending on the age and breed of the dog. This fee helps cover the cost of veterinary care, including vaccinations, spaying/neutering, microchipping, and any necessary medical treatments. It also contributes to the food, shelter, and care provided during their stay with us. Remember, adopting from a shelter is not only more affordable than purchasing from a breeder, but it also gives a deserving dog a second chance at happiness.
