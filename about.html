OUR MISSION

At Paws & Pups, our mission is to create a world where every dog has a loving home. Founded in 2015 by a group of passionate dog lovers, we've dedicated ourselves to rescuing, rehabilitating, and rehoming dogs in need. We believe that the bond between humans and dogs is one of the most special connections in the world, and we work tirelessly to help create these lifelong friendships.

OUR TEAM

Our dedicated team consists of veterinarians, animal behaviorists, trainers, and volunteers who share a common passion for canine welfare. Each team member brings unique skills and experiences, but we all share the same goal: to improve the lives of dogs and the people who love them. From medical care to behavioral training, our experts ensure that every dog receives the individualized attention they need to thrive.

OUR FACILITY

Our state-of-the-art facility provides a safe and comfortable environment for dogs awaiting their forever homes. With spacious play areas, cozy sleeping quarters, and a full veterinary clinic on-site, we ensure that all physical and emotional needs are met. We've created a stress-free space where dogs can socialize, exercise, and receive the love and care they deserve while they wait to meet their perfect match.
